## What Alfonso actually holds — recall vs. application, graded separately

This page records two genuinely different results from the same interrogation, and keeps them distinct rather than blending them into one rating, because they told different stories.

**Unprompted recall was weak.** Asked to state the equation cold, he named "risk" (effort/sacrifice, not yet distinguished as its own driver), a time-based factor, was unsure about a possible fifth component, and guessed the total count wrong (five, when the actual structure is four drivers — two increased, two decreased). He did not spontaneously produce "dream outcome" or "perceived likelihood of achievement" as separate named drivers.

**Applied understanding, once the framework was in front of him, was strong.** Given a real, personally relevant scenario — an AI receptionist offer for businesses, from his own stated interest in AI agency work — he correctly used three of the four drivers and reasoned about *why* each move works, not just naming them:

- **Dream outcome**: correctly identified the business's actual pain (losing potential customers/revenue from missed off-hours calls) and reframed the offer around that outcome rather than the product's mechanics.
- **Effort and sacrifice**: correctly reasoned that a business installing the receptionist bears near-zero effort or downside — "these are customers that would have been lost anyway, so the company doesn't lose anything."
- **Perceived likelihood of achievement**: correctly identified this as his own actual weak point to execute — that trust in the system would have to be built during the sales conversation itself, through explaining how it technically works — a self-assessed gap stated plainly rather than glossed over.

He did not apply **time delay** in this example — a real omission worth noting, not a big one.

## A note on how this page came about

Partway through this interrogation, the `value-equation-hormozi.md` page in `wiki/claude-knowledge/` got opened in his IDE, and he said so directly rather than letting it pass unremarked. Everything he said after that point stopped counting as evidence of independent recall — it's recorded above as a hard line. The application exercise that followed still counts as genuine evidence, since applying a framework correctly to a novel, specific case is a different (and harder) task than reading it back.
