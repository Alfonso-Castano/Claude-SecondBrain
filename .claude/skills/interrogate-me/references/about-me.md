# Applying interrogate-me: growing ABOUT-ME.md

Use this when the interrogation is meant to populate or grow `ABOUT-ME.md` — the
always-loaded, root-level file of always-true personal context about Alfonso, structurally
separate from the wiki proper.

## Cold start (the file is currently empty)

The first real session here should be an extensive, in-depth sweep, not a light pass:

1. **Start broad.** Cover Alfonso's life and work generally before narrowing — the goal at
   this stage is to find out what's worth going deep on, not to go deep immediately.
2. **Research before narrowing.** Before or during this broad sweep, actually research what
   tends to belong in a personal-context file for a system like this one — an AI that's
   meant to function as a genuinely knowledgeable co-founder / executive assistant, not
   just a topic-indexed archive. Let that research inform which specific topics get a deep
   pass, alongside your own reasoning from what comes up in the broad sweep.
3. **One topic is mandatory regardless of what the research turns up:** Alfonso's current
   business understanding and knowledge. The whole Second Brain revolves around this, so it
   gets a deep pass no matter what else the research surfaces.

## Ongoing growth (after the cold start)

ABOUT-ME.md is meant to keep growing, not stay static after one big session. Two paths,
both legitimate: smaller interrogate-me sessions later (a narrower follow-up on one life
area or a new topic that's come up), and ordinary conversation — Claude should fold in
what it learns about Alfonso through normal use, not only through deliberate sessions. This
reference file governs the deliberate-session path; the conversational path isn't this
skill's concern.

Per SKILL.md step 8's revise-don't-duplicate rule: if a later session touches something
ABOUT-ME.md already says, update that section in place rather than adding a second,
possibly-conflicting entry elsewhere in the file.

## Where the output goes, and whether it's gated

Write directly, ungated. ABOUT-ME.md is structurally outside the wiki (see its own header)
and was never covered by CLAUDE-WIKI.md §7's propose-then-confirm gate to begin with — it's
explicitly described as growing naturally and held to a lighter bar than wiki pages. SKILL.md
step 7's completeness checkpoint still applies before writing (show Alfonso the draft,
confirm it's enough) — that checkpoint is universal regardless of write-gating. It's the
write itself, after that checkpoint clears, that happens silently here.
